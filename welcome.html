Airplane Chime Timer

  • Timer is OFF by default - Click the extension icon and turn ON to start receiving chimes
  • Plays airplane chime sounds at regular intervals (15min, 30min, or 1hour) - example: 15:00, 15:30, 16:00
  • Set custom intervals (any minute value) for more flexible timing
  • Schedule chimes at specific times of day with daily repeat option
  • No popup notifications - audio only
  • Chimes exactly at :00 seconds of each interval
  • Upload and use your own custom chime sounds (MP3, WAV up to 1MB)
